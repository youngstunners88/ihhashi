## 3. FEE STRUCTURE AND PAYMENTS

### 3.1 Fee-Setting Freedom

**Your Right to Set Fees:**

You have the exclusive right to set your own fees on top of the base delivery rate:

- **Base Fee:** Calculated by iHhashi algorithm (distance, time, demand factors)
- **Your Fee:** Additional amount you set (can be positive, zero, or promotional/negative)
- **Total Fee:** Base Fee + Your Fee + Tips

**Fee Guidelines:**
- Set fees based on your costs, time, and desired earnings
- Consider distance, traffic, weather, and order size
- Adjust fees dynamically based on demand
- Competitive fees may increase order frequency

### 3.2 Customer Choice Model

Our Platform uses a transparent bidding system:

1. Customer places order
2. Available Partners see order details
3. Each Partner submits offer (Your Fee + estimated time)
4. Customer reviews all offers
5. Customer selects preferred Partner based on price and time
6. Selected Partner receives order confirmation

**Implications:**
- Lower fees may increase selection probability
- Faster delivery times improve competitiveness
- Rating and Blue Horse status influence customer choice
- You may decline orders without penalty

### 3.3 Payment Terms

**Earnings Components:**
- Base Fee (algorithm-calculated)
- Your Fee (self-set)
- Tips (100% yours, 0% platform fee)
- Promotional bonuses

**Payment Schedule:**
- Weekly payouts every Sunday at 11:11 AM SAST
- Minimum payout: R100 (accumulates if below threshold)
- Direct deposit to your registered South African bank account
- Payment reference: "iHhashi - [Week Ending Date]"

**Deductions:**
- Platform service fee (percentage of Base Fee only)
- Refunds for cancelled/failed deliveries
- Penalties for violations (after fair process)
- No deductions from tips (guaranteed 0%)

**Fee Statement:**
- Detailed breakdown provided weekly
- Shows each delivery, fees, tips, deductions
- Available in-app and via email
- Retained for 5 years

### 3.4 45-Day Free Trial

New Partners receive:
- 45 calendar days free platform access
- No subscription fees
- No commission on first 10 deliveries
- Full access to all Platform features
- After trial: Standard platform fees apply

## 5. PLATFORM RULES

### 5.1 Order Acceptance

**Accepting Orders:**
- Review order details before accepting
- Ensure you can complete within estimated time
- Consider your current location and route
- You may decline any order without penalty

**After Acceptance:**
- Proceed immediately to merchant
- Communicate delays to customer via app
- Do not cancel after collection except emergencies
- Follow delivery instructions precisely

### 5.2 Cancellations

**Valid Cancellation Reasons:**
- Customer requests cancellation
- Merchant unable to fulfill order
- Safety concerns (weather, location, personal safety)
- Vehicle breakdown (documented)
- Medical emergency

**Invalid Cancellations:**
- Found better-paying order
- Customer location too far (you accepted knowing distance)
- Simply changed your mind
- Discriminatory reasons

**Consequences:**
- First invalid cancellation: Warning
- Second: 24-hour suspension
- Third: 7-day suspension
- Pattern of cancellations: Account termination

### 5.3 Customer Interaction

**Communication:**
- Use in-app messaging for all communication
- Call customer only if necessary and brief
- Be professional and courteous
- Do not share personal contact details

**Delivery:**
- Verify recipient identity for age-restricted items
- Obtain signature or photo proof of delivery
- Follow "leave at door" instructions with photo
- Return undeliverable items to merchant

## 8. TERMINATION

### 8.1 Termination by Partner

You may terminate this Agreement at any time:
- Provide 7 days written notice
- Complete accepted orders
- Return any iHhashi property
- Outstanding earnings paid at next payout

### 8.2 Termination by iHhashi

**Immediate Termination (no notice):**
- Theft or fraud
- Violence or threats
- Serious traffic violations while on delivery
- Sharing customer data
- Impersonating another person
- Criminal conviction relevant to role

**Termination with Notice:**
- Persistent poor performance (after improvement plan)
- Repeated policy violations
- Extended inactivity (90+ days)
- 7 days notice provided

### 8.3 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Platform access revoked immediately
- Outstanding earnings paid (minus deductions)
- Delivery bag need not be returned (yours to keep)
- Rating and history deleted per data retention policy
- No re-employment or compensation claims

### 8.4 Appeal Process

If terminated, you may appeal within 14 days:
- Email appeals@ihhashi.co.za
- Provide evidence and explanation
- Independent review within 21 days
- Decision is final
