## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

### 2.1 Platform Nature

iHhashi operates as a **technology platform** that connects:
- **Customers** seeking delivery of goods
- **Merchants** selling food, groceries, fresh produce, and other items
- **Delivery Partners** providing transportation and delivery services

**We do not provide delivery services ourselves.** We facilitate connections between independent parties.

### 2.2 Service Categories

The Platform enables delivery of:
- Prepared food and beverages
- Groceries and household items
- Fresh produce (fruits, vegetables, dairy)
- Personal courier services
- Other items from partner merchants

### 2.3 How It Works

1. Customer places order through Platform
2. Merchant prepares the order
3. Delivery Partners submit offers (fee + time estimate)
4. Customer selects preferred Delivery Partner
5. Delivery Partner collects and delivers order
6. Payment processed through Platform

### 2.4 Pricing Model

**Base Delivery Fee:** Calculated by algorithm considering distance, time, and demand (competitive with market rates).

**Partner-Set Fees:** Delivery Partners may set additional fees on top of base rate.

**Customer Choice:** Customers see multiple offers and select based on price and delivery time.
