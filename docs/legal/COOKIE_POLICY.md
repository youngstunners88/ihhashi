## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions.

**Types of Cookies:**
- **First-party cookies:** Set by iHhashi directly
- **Third-party cookies:** Set by our service providers and partners
- **Session cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

## 5. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit ihhashi.co.za, you will see a cookie consent banner where you can:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences
- Learn more about each cookie type

### 5.2 Changing Your Preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time:

**Via our Platform:**
1. Go to Account → Settings → Privacy
2. Click on "Cookie Preferences"
3. Adjust your settings
4. Save changes

**Via your browser:**

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Manage cookies or block third-party cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Manage permissions or clear data

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Manage cookies and website data

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage cookies or clear browsing data

### 5.3 What Happens If You Reject Cookies?

**Essential cookies cannot be disabled** - the Platform will not function without them.

If you disable non-essential cookies:
- ✅ Platform will still work
- ✅ You can place orders
- ⚠️ You'll need to re-enter preferences each visit
- ⚠️ Personalized recommendations won't work
- ⚠️ You may see less relevant content
- ⚠️ We can't improve based on your usage patterns
