## 🎯 UNIQUE BUSINESS MODEL COVERAGE

### inDriver-Style Pricing Model

All documents consistently explain:

**Base Fee:** Algorithm-calculated (distance, time, demand)
**Partner Fee:** Set independently by each delivery partner
**Customer Choice:** Multiple offers shown, customer selects based on price/time

**Referenced In:**
- Terms of Service: Section 2.4, 3.4, 5.2
- Partner Agreement: Section 3.1, 3.2
- Customer Terms: Section 3.3, 4.1

### Multi-Modal Transport

Five transport modes with specific requirements:

| Mode | Base Fee | Max Distance | Requirements |
|------|----------|--------------|--------------|
| Walking | R15 | 2km | Fitness, shoes |
| Skateboard | R18 | 5km | Board, protective gear |
| Bicycle | R20 | 10km | Roadworthy bike, helmet |
| Motorbike | R30 | 25km | License, insurance, helmet |
| Car | R50 | 50km | License, insurance |

**Documented In:** Partner Agreement Section 4.1

### Blue Horse Verification

Three-tier verification system:

| Level | Requirements | Benefits |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| Basic | ID, phone | Standard access |
| Verified | + Photo, license, background check | Badge, priority |
| Premium | + Interview, references | Lowest fees, top priority |

**Documented In:** Partner Agreement Section 6.1
