## 3. PLACING ORDERS

### 3.1 How Ordering Works

1. **Browse:** View available merchants in your area
2. **Select:** Choose items and add to cart
3. **Review:** Check order details, delivery address, and total cost
4. **Confirm:** Place order and wait for acceptance
5. **Choose Delivery Partner:** Review offers and select preferred partner
6. **Track:** Monitor delivery in real-time
7. **Receive:** Accept delivery and confirm receipt

### 3.2 Order Acceptance

**Merchant Acceptance:**
- Merchants have 10 minutes to accept or decline
- If declined, you receive immediate notification and refund
- Prices may vary slightly for fresh items (market prices)

**Your Acceptance of Delivery Partner:**
- See multiple partner offers with fees and delivery times
- Choose based on your preference (price vs. speed)
- No obligation to choose lowest price

### 3.3 Order Modifications

**Before Acceptance:**
- Cancel order: Full refund
- Modify items: Edit cart and resubmit

**After Acceptance:**
- Contact merchant directly for modifications
- Significant changes may require cancellation and reorder
- No modifications after partner collection

## 5. DELIVERY

### 5.1 Delivery Areas

We deliver to areas where partner coverage exists. Enter your address to check availability.

### 5.2 Delivery Timeframes

**Estimated Times:**
- Fast Food: 25-45 minutes
- Groceries: 45-90 minutes
- Fresh Produce: 30-60 minutes
- Courier: Varies by distance

**Factors Affecting Delivery Time:**
- Distance from merchant
- Traffic conditions
- Order preparation time
- Time of day (peak hours)
- Weather conditions

**Important:** Estimated times are not guarantees. Actual times may vary.

### 5.3 Delivery Process

**At Your Door:**
- Delivery partner will call or message upon arrival
- You or authorized person must accept delivery
- ID check for age-restricted items
- Inspect items before confirming receipt

**If You're Not Available:**
- Partner will wait up to 5 minutes
- May leave items in safe place (if instructed)
- Photo proof taken
- Second delivery attempt may incur additional fee

### 5.4 Delivery Instructions

You can provide special instructions:
- Gate codes or access information
- "Leave at door" requests
- Specific entry instructions
- Contact preferences

**Note:** Partners are not required to:
- Enter homes or buildings
- Climb stairs above ground floor
- Wait extended periods
- Perform tasks beyond delivery

### 5.5 Contactless Delivery

Available option:
- Partner leaves items at specified location
- Photo confirmation sent
- No signature required
- You assume risk of theft/damage after placement

## 6. CANCELLATIONS AND REFUNDS

### 6.1 Cancellation by Customer

| Timing | Refund Amount |
|--------|---------------|
| Before merchant acceptance | 100% |
| After acceptance, before partner assigned | 100% |
| After partner assigned, before collection | 50% |
| After partner collection | 0% (food safety) |

**How to Cancel:**
- Via app: Order → Cancel Order
- Or contact support: support@ihhashi.co.za

### 6.2 Cancellation by Merchant

If merchant cancels:
- Full refund automatically processed
- SMS and email notification
- Option to reorder from different merchant

### 6.3 Cancellation by Delivery Partner

If partner cancels after assignment:
- New partner assigned automatically
- Or you may choose new partner
- No charge if unable to complete

### 6.4 Refund Policy

**Full Refunds For:**
- Order never delivered
- Items significantly different from order
- Spoiled or inedible food
- Wrong items delivered

**Partial Refunds For:**
- Missing items (refund value of missing items)
- Incorrect sizes (difference in price)
- Quality issues (assessed case-by-case)

**No Refunds For:**
- Change of mind after delivery
- Late delivery (unless excessively delayed)
- Minor presentation issues
- Items you simply don't like (taste preference)

### 6.5 Refund Process

1. Request refund via app within 24 hours
2. Provide reason and photos if applicable
3. Investigation within 48 hours
4. Refund to original payment method
5. Processing time: 5-10 business days

### 6.6 CPA Cooling-Off Right

Under the Consumer Protection Act, you may cancel:
- Within 5 business days for services not yet rendered
- Full refund guaranteed
- No questions asked
