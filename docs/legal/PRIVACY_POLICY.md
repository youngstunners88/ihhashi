## 1. INTRODUCTION

iHhashi ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our delivery platform at ihhashi.co.za and our mobile applications (collectively, the "Platform").

This Privacy Policy complies with the **Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA)** of South Africa and other applicable data protection laws.

By using our Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following categories of personal information:

#### **Account Information**
- Full name and surname
- South African cellphone number
- Email address
- Date of birth
- Profile photograph

#### **Delivery Information**
- Delivery addresses (home, work, saved locations)
- GPS location data (real-time during delivery)
- Delivery instructions and preferences
- Alternative contact numbers

#### **Payment Information**
- Bank account details (for delivery partners)
- Card information (processed securely via Paystack)
- Payment history and transaction records
- VAT invoices

#### **Device and Usage Information**
- IP address
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type and version
- App usage statistics
- Cookies and similar technologies

### 2.2 Information from Delivery Partners

For delivery partners ("iHhashi Heroes"), we additionally collect:

- South African ID number or passport number
- Driver's license details (where applicable)
- Vehicle registration and number plate
- Vehicle photographs
- Proof of address
- Banking details for payments
- Criminal record check consent
- Verification documents (Blue Horse verification)

### 2.3 Information from Merchants

For merchant partners, we collect:

- Business registration documents
- CIPC registration number
- Tax clearance certificate
- Business address and contact details
- Menu and pricing information
- Banking details for settlements

## 3. HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION

We collect information through:

1. **Direct Collection:** When you register, place orders, or update your profile
2. **Automated Collection:** GPS tracking, cookies, device information
3. **Third Parties:** Payment processors, verification services, background check providers
4. **Merchants:** Order details, product information

## 5. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### 5.1 Primary Uses

- **Matching:** Connecting customers with available delivery partners
- **Delivery:** Providing real-time tracking and delivery updates
- **Payments:** Processing payments and paying delivery partners
- **Communication:** Order updates, promotional offers (if opted in), support
- **Verification:** Blue Horse verification for trust and safety
- **Quality Control:** Monitoring service standards

### 5.2 Location Data

Your location is used to:
- Show available merchants in your area
- Calculate delivery distances and times
- Track delivery progress in real-time
- Match you with nearby delivery partners

You can disable location services, but this will limit Platform functionality.

## 7. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups with geographic redundancy
- **Audits:** Regular security assessments and penetration testing

### Security Incident Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
1. Notify the Information Regulator within 72 hours
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours where required by law
3. Take immediate steps to mitigate harm
4. Provide guidance on protective measures

---

## 8. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary:

| Data Category | Retention Period |
|---------------|------------------|
| Account information | Duration of account + 5 years |
| Order history | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Payment records | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Location data | 90 days |
| Marketing preferences | Until withdrawal of consent |
| Deleted accounts | 3 years (legal obligations) |

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 9. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER POPIA

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### 9.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of your personal information we hold.

### 9.2 Right to Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

### 9.3 Right to Deletion
Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).

### 9.4 Right to Object
Object to processing for direct marketing or legitimate interests.

### 9.5 Right to Restriction
Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

### 9.6 Right to Data Portability
Request your data in a machine-readable format.

### 9.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for optional processing (e.g., marketing).

### 9.8 Right to Complain
Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator:
- Website: www.inforegulator.org.za
- Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za
