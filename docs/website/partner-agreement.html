Delivery Partner Agreement

Effective Date: 1 March 2026

1. PARTNERSHIP OVERVIEW

This Agreement governs the relationship between iHhashi (Pty) Ltd ("Company") and independent delivery partners ("Partner" or "You").

2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

You are an independent contractor, not an employee. You are responsible for:

  • Your own taxes and insurance
  • Maintaining your vehicle/equipment
  • Compliance with traffic laws
  • Obtaining necessary licenses and permits

3. REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Vehicle Requirements

  • Roadworthy vehicle (car, motorcycle, scooter, bicycle, or on-foot)
  • Valid driver's license (where applicable)
  • Valid vehicle registration and insurance

3.2 Personal Requirements

  • Valid South African ID
  • Clear criminal record
  • Smartphone with data
  • Bank account for payments

4. PAYMENTS

  • Partners set their own delivery rates competitively
  • Payouts every Sunday at 11:11 AM SAST
  • Minimum payout: R100
  • 100% of tips go to partners
  • 45-day free trial for new partners

5. CONDUCT STANDARDS

Partners must:

  • Maintain professional behavior
  • Handle orders with care
  • Follow food safety guidelines
  • Wear appropriate attire
  • Respect customer property

6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate with 7 days notice. Immediate termination for:

  • Criminal activity
  • Repeated customer complaints
  • Fraud or theft
  • Violent behavior

7. LIABILITY

You are responsible for your actions while delivering. Maintain adequate insurance coverage.

8. CONTACT

Partner Support: partners@ihhashi.co.za
