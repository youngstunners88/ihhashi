1. **JHB Launch** - Sandton, Rosebank, Braamfontein first (high-density, tech-savvy)

3. **Township Expansion** - Soweto, Alexandra, Tembisa with cash-on-delivery
4. **Referral Loops** - WhatsApp-native sharing (SA's primary messenger)

6. **Merchant Growth** - 45-day free trial → 15% platform fee conversion

## SA Market Context
- WhatsApp is the #1 communication channel (not SMS, not email)
- Data costs matter - minimize app size and data usage
- Airtime rewards are highly valued in the informal economy
- Community trust is essential - word-of-mouth > paid ads in townships
- Load-shedding creates ordering spikes (people order when they can't cook)
