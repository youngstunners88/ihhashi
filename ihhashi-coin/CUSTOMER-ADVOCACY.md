# iHhashi Coin: Customer Incentive Model Analysis

**Author:** Customer Advocate (Zo)  
**Date:** 2026-03-02  
**Purpose:** Design the best incentive model to create customer LOYALTY and HOLDING pressure

---

## Executive Summary

The current 100,000 iHhashi Coin threshold for Gold Horse status is **too high** for regular customers. At R0.10 per coin, that's R10,000 (≈$550 USD) — beyond the reach of most South African consumers. We need a **tiered approach** with achievable milestones that create psychological ownership and FOMO (fear of missing out).

---

## 1. Why Would a Regular Person Hold iHhashi Coins?

### The Core Problem
Most loyalty points are seen as "free money to spend" — users accumulate and immediately redeem. There's no holding pressure because the points have no market value and no appreciation potential.

### The iHhashi Coin Difference
iHhashi Coins are **tradeable** on Zora. This creates a fundamental shift:

| Traditional Points | iHhashi Coins |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Zero market value | Real market value (can sell for ETH) |
| Issued by company | On-chain, transparent supply |
| Inflationary (company prints more) | Deflationary (burn on redemption) |
| No price discovery | Price determined by market |
| No speculation | Speculative upside potential |

### Why Hold Instead of Redeem?

**1. Speculative Upside**
- "What if iHhashi Coin goes from R0.10 to R1.00? My 500 coins become R500!"
- This is the crypto-native hook — FOMO on potential appreciation

**2. Status Benefits (Non-Financial)**
- Gold Horse status could mean: priority delivery, exclusive vendors, lower fees
- Status is **cumulative** — you need to MAINTAIN holdings, not just reach once

**3. Ongoing Passive Income**
- Trade referrers earn 4% of trading fees
- If you refer a friend who buys R1000 worth, you earn R40 in referral fees
- This creates a network effect — you want to refer AND hold

**4. Deflationary Pressure**
- Every redemption BURNS coins
- As coins are burned, remaining coins become more scarce
- Early holders benefit from scarcity

## 2. Tier Structure: Beyond the 100K Gold Horse

### Proposed Customer Tiers (HOLDING-Based)

| Tier | Coins Required | Monthly Value | % of Customers | Benefits |
|------|---------------|---------------|----------------|----------|
| **Brown Pony** 🐴 | 100 | R10 | 60% | 2% off orders |
| **Silver Mustang** 🐎 | 500 | R50 | 25% | 5% off + 1 free delivery/quarter |
| **Gold Horse** 🏇 | 5,000 | R500 | 10% | 10% off + priority delivery + exclusive vendors |
| **Platinum Stallion** 🦄 | 25,000 | R2,500 | 4% | 15% off + unlimited free delivery + VIP support |
| **Diamond Unicorn** 💎 | 100,000 | R10,000 | 1% | 20% off + merchant pricing + equity pool |

### Key Design Principles

1. **Accessible Entry**: Brown Pony at 100 coins (R10) — anyone can reach this
2. **Meaningful Middle**: Gold Horse at 5,000 coins (R500) — achievable for loyal customers
3. **Aspirational Top**: 100K remains prestigious but not the only goal
4. **Cumulative Status**: Must MAINTAIN holdings to keep status (anti-dump mechanism)

### Why This Works

- **Brown Pony** creates the "first step" — once you have 100 coins, you're invested
- **Silver Mustang** is the sweet spot — R500 investment is meaningful but achievable
- **Gold Horse** at 5K coins (R500) is REALISTIC for a loyal customer who orders weekly
- **Platinum/Diamond** tiers remain aspirational for power users and merchants

**Gold Horse Exclusive Vendors:**
- Premium restaurants that only serve Gold Horse+ members
- Limited edition menu items

**Platinum Stallion Perks:**

**The math works**: Holding R500 worth of coins (Gold Horse) saves R130/month = 26% annual ROI on coins held. This creates REAL holding pressure.

## 4. Referral Integration: Earning Coins + Trading Fees

### Current System (Web2)
- Referrer: 50 iHhashi Coins per referral
- New customer: 25 iHhashi Coins welcome bonus

### Proposed Enhanced System (Web2 + Web3)

**Level 1: Basic Referral (No Wallet Required)**
- Referrer earns 50 Web2 iHhashi Coins
- New customer gets 25 Web2 iHhashi Coins
- Works exactly like current system

**Level 2: On-Chain Referral (Wallet Required)**
- Referrer earns 50 Web2 iHhashi Coins + becomes **Trade Referrer** on Zora
- When the new customer buys iHhashi Coins on Zora, referrer earns 4% of trading fees
- This creates **ongoing passive income** — not just a one-time bonus

**Level 3: Merchant/Delivery Serviceman Referral**
- When a customer refers a merchant or delivery serviceman who then buys iHhashi Coins
- Customer earns 4% of ALL their trading fees forever
- This is the "Golden Ticket" — if you refer a popular merchant who trades heavily, you earn perpetually

### Example Scenario

```
Customer A refers Customer B:
- Customer A gets 50 iHhashi Coins (Web2)
- Customer B gets 25 iHhashi Coins (Web2)

Customer B decides to buy R1000 of iHhashi Coins on Zora:
- Trading fee (1%): R10
- Trade Referrer (Customer A) earns: R0.40 (4% of R10)
- iHhashi earns: R2.00 (20% of R10)

Customer B later sells R2000 of iHhashi Coins:
- Trading fee (1%): R20
- Trade Referrer (Customer A) earns: R0.80 (4% of R20)
- Customer A didn't do anything extra, but earned R1.20 total

If Customer B becomes a regular trader, Customer A earns passively forever.
```

## 6. Anti-Dump Mechanisms

### The Problem
If coins are too easy to sell, everyone dumps. We need reasons to HOLD.

### Solutions

**1. Vesting for Earned Coins**
- Coins earned from referrals/challenges: 50% available immediately, 50% vested over 30 days
- Creates anticipation and continued engagement

**2. Status Decay**
- Status tiers checked monthly
- If holdings drop below threshold, status downgrades
- Creates "subscription-like" holding behavior

**3. Redemption Penalty**
- Redeeming coins for discounts: 1:1 value (100 coins = R10 discount)
- Selling on Zora: market value (could be higher or lower)
- User chooses: guaranteed discount OR speculative sale

**4. Burn Mechanism**
- Every redemption BURNS the coins
- Reduces supply, increases scarcity
- Holders benefit from others redeeming

**5. Holding Rewards**
- Bonus coins for maintaining tier for 3, 6, 12 months
- "Anniversary bonuses" for long-term holders

## Conclusion

**The key insight**: For customers to HOLD iHhashi Coins, they need **multiple compelling reasons**:

1. **Utility** — Discounts and free delivery (immediate value)
2. **Status** — Tiers with exclusive benefits (social proof)
3. **Speculation** — Potential price appreciation (greed/FOMO)
4. **Passive Income** — Trade referral fees (ongoing rewards)
5. **Gamification** — Streaks, badges, challenges (engagement)
6. **Scarcity** — Burn mechanism (deflation)

By lowering the Gold Horse threshold from 100,000 to 5,000 coins and creating intermediate tiers, we make holding achievable for regular customers while maintaining aspirational goals for power users.

The combination of **immediate utility** + **speculative upside** + **passive income** creates a unique value proposition that traditional loyalty programs cannot match.
