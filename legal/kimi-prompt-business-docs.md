## Pricing Model (Include in All Relevant Documents)

### Hybrid Fee Structure
1. **Base Fee** - Calculated by algorithm (distance, time, demand)
   - Competitive benchmarking: Uber Eats, Bolt Food, Mr D, Checkers Sixty60
   - Transparent calculation shown to customers

2. **Partner-Set Fee** - Delivery partners ADD their own fee
   - Partners compete for orders
   - Customers see multiple offers with different prices and ETAs
   - Similar to inDriver's bidding model, adapted for delivery

3. **Customer Choice**
   - Customer sees all available delivery offers
   - Selects based on: price, delivery time, partner rating
   - Clear breakdown: base fee + partner fee = total delivery cost
